Mid-Season Grades

Raptors Raptors Among NBA's Elite

Posted by Zain Murtaza on January 5th, 2016
Now that we have officially crossed the midseason of the NBA season, it is fair enough to state that barring any drastic roster changes, the Toronto Raptors are what their record suggests. At a record of 35-16, this Raptor team is statistically the best in franchise history. Not only does this team win, but it wins in ways that may finally translate into a deep playoff run. After disappointing all of Canada last year by being swept by the Washington Wizards, the Raptors have gone back to what turned this franchise around 2 years ago; defense. The Raptors thus far, have managed to be a team that is top 5 in defense and offensive efficiency. What does this mean? This implies that the Raptors are playing both ends of the court, getting critical stops when needed and are capable enough to score the ball through their two All Stars. To put things into perspective, there are only four other teams in the NBA that rank top 5 in both offensive and defensive efficiency. The Oklahoma City Thunder, Golden State Warriors, Cleveland Cavaliers, San Antonio Spurs and your Toronto Raptors. Get excited Canada, you got a good one this year!
    Grade: A
