Officials Blunder Cost Raptors an Opportunity in Regulation

Raptors Fall to Hornets in Overtime

Posted by Zain Murtaza on January 1st, 2016

Charlotte has never played the role of a kind host to the Raptors, as the Raptors once again fell to the Hornets last night. However, this loss comes as controversial as it can get. And boy do we know about Controversy. Once again the officials made a series of blunders that played in a role to the Raptors overtime loss in Charlotte. From the video shown below, there are a series of events that you can notice:

  1. Officials ignore Kyle Lowry's initial timeout call at 3 seconds right when DeMar stole the ball
  2. Officials call a Timeout with 0.7 sec left, when DeMar is in the midst of his shot.
  3. They don't put any time back on the game clock causing the Raptors to force up a near impossible shot with 0.7 seconds left. When watching the replay of the video, it was clear the Raptors should have been given near 2 seconds.

Just a really poor display of officiating that had cost the Raptors a chance to win the game in the dying seconds of regulation
