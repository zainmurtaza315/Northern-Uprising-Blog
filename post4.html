Trade Deadline Speculation

Raptors Destined to Make a Move!

Posted by Zain Murtaza on January 4th, 2016

With the season now passed its midway point, rumors and speculation heat up as the trade deadline nears. Last year after tallying up the most wins they had ever had before All Star break, the 2014 Raptors fell apart, stumbling the rest of the season to a mark below .500 and cost the team an opportunity at its first 50-win campaign in franchise history. Raptors look to avoid that this year as there have been multiple reports that the Raptors have strong interest in the Suns Forward Markief Morris. Morris, although having a down year, may be the offensive punch the Raptors may be looking for off of the bench. Not only would the trade help the Raptors right now, but it may be worth the risk due to Morris’s friendly contract. Morris is not the only forward the Raptors are keeping an eye on however. Nuggets Forward Kennth Faried is another name tied to the Raptors in trade rumors. Faried, a forward who was drafted by Raptors GM Masai Ujiri could very well end up in Toronto due to Masai’s relationship with the Nuggets and previous history. All signs indicate that the Raptors are gearing up to make a move in the next couple of weeks. Could a deadline day deal be the final move to take the Raptors over the top?
